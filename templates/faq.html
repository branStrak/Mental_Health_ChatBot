Frequently Asked Questions

What is MannTalk?

MannTalk is an AI-powered mental health chatbot designed to offer 24/7 support, reliable guidance, and compassionate care to help you manage your mental health and overall well-being.

How secure and private is my data?

Your privacy is our top priority. All conversations with MannTalk are fully encrypted and confidential. We strictly follow privacy guidelines and consistently update our security measures.

Can I talk to a human instead?

Currently, MannTalk is fully powered by advanced artificial intelligence designed to simulate empathetic conversation. However, we continually explore ways to integrate human support when extra assistance is needed.

What types of support does MannTalk provide?

MannTalk offers emotional support, stress reduction techniques, mindfulness exercises, and advice on coping strategies. Our aim is to empower you to feel heard and to assist you in managing your mental well-being.

How do I start a chat with MannTalk?

Simply click the "Start Chat" button found on any page. This will open our interactive chat window where you can begin your conversation with MannTalk instantly.
