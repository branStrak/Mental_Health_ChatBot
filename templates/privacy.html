Our Promise to Your Privacy

At MannTalk, your well-being and trust are at the forefront of our mission. We are dedicated to maintaining a safe, compassionate, and secure online environment. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. What We Collect

We gather information to improve your experience, including:

  • Account Data: Personal details such as name, email, phone, and username you provide during registration.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with our website and chat services.
  • Feedback: Your inputs, survey responses, and any additional information you choose to share with us.

2. Use of Your Information

Your data is utilized for:

  • Personalizing and enhancing your experience with our services.
  • Improving our systems based on data-driven insights.
  • Communicating important updates, support messages, and wellness tips.
  • Maintaining security and operational integrity.

3. Data Security

We implement state-of-the-art security measures—including encryption and secure servers—to protect your information from unauthorized access, modification, or disclosure.

4. Your Control & Rights

You are empowered to manage your personal data. You can access, update, or request deletion of your information, and opt-out of non-critical data collection.

5. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Notice of significant changes will be provided through our website or your registered email.

6. Reach Out to Us

For any privacy concerns or questions, please contact us using the details below:

  • Email: support@MannTalk.com
  • Phone: +91 9876543210

Your trust is our asset. We are committed to safeguarding your personal information with the utmost care.
